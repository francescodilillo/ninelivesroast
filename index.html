Our Story

Born from a love of coffee & cats

Nine Lives Roast was founded in late 2025. A passion project started with specialty coffee and a deep love for cats (especially mine), which is why they're all over the logo.

We partner with a local Amsterdam roaster to source beans ethically from Peru, Colombia, and Brazil, and roast them through CO₂-neutral processes. The result is delicious, sustainable coffee that doesn't ask you to compromise on either.

Running as a solo eenmanszaak keeps things small and personal: every bag that reaches you has been thought about, not just processed.

What we stand for

Good coffee doesn't cost the earth

🌍
CO₂-Neutral
We roast through CO₂-neutral processes and work with partners who take the same approach seriously. It's part of how we do things, not a badge we wear.
🤝
Transparent Sourcing
We publish exactly where your money goes and farmers get the majority. Our importer, This Side Up Coffees, and our roaster, Specialty Roast, work directly with producers to keep the chain short and honest.

Made with Care
This is a one-person operation. Every decision, from bean selection to the logo, is personal. That's not a limitation, it's the whole point.
